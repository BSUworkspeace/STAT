Robust parameter estimation, using M estimation

M-estimation defines a weight function such that the estimating equation becomes \(\sum_{i=1}^{n} w_{i}\left(y_{i}-x^{\prime} b\right) x_{i}^{\prime}=0\) . But the weights depend on the residuals and the residuals on the weights

MM=rlm(Consumption ~ Income + Production + Unemployment + Savings,
    data=uschange)
summary(MM)
